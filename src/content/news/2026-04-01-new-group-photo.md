Our new group picture was taken during the 2026 CompMet team outing, and it felt like the perfect moment to look back.

Comparing our 2023 and 2026 group photos, a lot has changed in three years. Some familiar faces have remained, many new faces have joined, and together the group has continued to grow in both size and spirit. What has stayed the same is the curiosity that brings us together: a shared passion for science, collaboration, and discovering new things as a team.

![CompMet group photo 2023](/images/news/group_picture_2023.png)

![CompMet group photo 2026](/images/news/group_picture_2026.jpg)

Left to right in the 2026 group picture: Ioannis Kontogiannis, Jorick van IJcken, Joelle Mergola Greef, Niek de Jonge, Justin van der Hooft, Robert Koetsier, Felicia Wolters, Jonas Dietrich, Esteban Charria Giron, Larissa Della Vedova, Paula Derksen Macruz, and Elena Ferri.

We were also sorry to miss Elena Del Pup, Laura Rosina Torres Ortega, Annette Lien, H.M. Suraj, and Nicky van der Wielen, who were abroad or could not make it that day.
